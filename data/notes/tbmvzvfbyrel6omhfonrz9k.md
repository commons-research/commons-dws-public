# [Kendrick Mass (wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kendrick_mass)
- **Kendrick mass**: Defined as setting the mass of a molecular fragment to an `Da` (dalton). 
- **[Dalton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dalton_(unit))**: `Da` symbol, unit of mass, defined as $\frac{1}{12}$ of the mass of carbon-12. 
## Definition
The mass of $CH_2$ is defined as `14 Da`, rather than the IUPAC value of `14.01565 Da`. The conversion is done as:
$$Kendrick mass = {IUPAC} {mass} \times \frac{14.00000}{14.01565}$$
## Kendrick mass defect
The exact Kendrick mass subtracted from the nominal Kendrick mass:
$$\text{Kendrick mass defect} = \text{nominal Kendrick mass - Kendrick mass}$$
But due to rounding errors has become:
$$\text{Kendrick mass defect} = \text{nominal mass} - \text{Kendrick exact mass}$$
