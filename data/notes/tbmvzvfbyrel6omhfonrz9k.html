The mass of CH2CH_2CH2​ is defined as 14 Da, rather than the IUPAC value of 14.01565 Da. The conversion is done as: Kendrickmass=IUPACmass×14.0000014.01565Kendrick mass = {IUPAC} {mass} \times \frac{14.00000}{14.01565}Kendrickmass=IUPACmass×14.0156514.00000​
