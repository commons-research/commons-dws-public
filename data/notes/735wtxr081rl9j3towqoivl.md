# [Aromatic Compounds (wiki)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aromatic_compound)

Typically built around a [benzene](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aromatic_compound), and cyclically conjugated. Aromatic is now a misnomer and has name from historical use. Properties:
- Typically unreaactive
- non polar and hydrophobic
- high carbon-hydrogen ratios
- burn with a sooty yellow flame
- can undergo electrophilic substitution reactions and nucleophilic  aromatic substitutions
Broken into two categories: 
1) Benzene ring modelled
2) non-benzoids
