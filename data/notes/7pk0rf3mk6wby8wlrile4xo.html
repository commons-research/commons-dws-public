As there are no universal description of what a tree is, I will propose a first definition for this bachelor work. A plant referred to as a tree is perennial, is lignified, has a trunk. Encompasses palms, cycadales, conifers, arbres standards, bonsais. But excludes banana trees, papaya trees, shrubs
