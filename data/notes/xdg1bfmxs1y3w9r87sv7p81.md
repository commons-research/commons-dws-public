# Trigocherrin A, the First NaturalChlorinated Daphnane Diterpene Orthoester from Trigonostemon cherrieri
## Terms

- **Daphnane diterpenoid orthoester (DDO)**: A diterpene natural product class with a complex oxygenated polycyclic structure and orthoester group.
- **Organohalogen**: Organic molecule containing halogen atoms, such as chlorine.
- **HRESIMS**: High-resolution electrospray ionization mass spectrometry. 
- **Isotope pattern**: MS peak pattern caused by natural isotope abundances. 
- **NMR**: Nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy. Used to determine molecular structure and atom connectivity.
## Notes
HRESIMS gave the molecular formula:
`C38H36Cl2O12`
Observed `[M+H]+` and `[M+H+2]+` peaks with a ratio consistent with chlorine atoms.

> isotope pattern helps identify halogens, not just molecular mass.

For atom identification work, this is useful because chlorine is detectable from isotope signatures due to natural isotope abundance.

Findings:
1. chlorine atoms can be inferred from isotope pattern
2. **fluorine** lacks the same isotope pattern advantage
3. molecular formula prediction should account for halogens differently
4. mass spec alone may identify formula but not complete structure
5. structural class/source metadata can act as useful priors
6. natural product papers contain rich graph relations
7. artifact checks are important for halogenated molecules
