Frictionless Py

A data-management framework for Python that provides functionality for working with tabular data - DEVT framework.

Purpose

  • describe your data
  • extract your data
  • validate your data
  • transform your data

Features

  • open source (MIT)
  • powerful
  • CLI
  • low resource use
  • supports compression
  • over 1k tests
