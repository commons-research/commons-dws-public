# [Aliphatic Compound (wiki)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aliphatic_compound)

Represent a class of hydrocarbons, distinct from [[aromatic compounds]], which can be saturated (`C-C` bonds are all single and hydrogens are needed). Aliphatic bonds can also be unsaturated with double bonds or triple bonds.
