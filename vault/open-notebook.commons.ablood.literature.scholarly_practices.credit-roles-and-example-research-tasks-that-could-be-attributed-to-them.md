# [Contributor Role Taxonomy (CRediT)](https://credit.niso.org/)
14 contributor roles
## [CRediT roles and example research tasks that could be attributed to them](https://zenodo.org/records/18421449)

### Conceptualization
Developing the overall research ideas, questions and goals.
Examples:
- identifying research problems
- developing hypotheses
- designing research frameworks
- refining project aims
### Data Curation
Managing, organizing and preserving research data and metadata.
Examples:
- cleaning and annotating data
- integrating datasets
- maintaining metadata
- version control and archiving
- ensuring data is reusable (FAIR)
### Formal Analysis
Applying mathematical, statistical or computational methods to analyze data.
Examples:
- statistical analysis
- machine learning
- computational modeling
- identifying patterns and relationships
### Funding Acquisition
Obtaining financial support for the project.
Examples:
- identifying funding opportunities
- writing grant proposals
- preparing budgets
### Investigation
Performing the research and collecting evidence.
Examples:
- conducting experiments
- collecting data
- literature review
- documenting findings
### Methodology
Designing the methods used in the research.
Examples:
- developing analytical methods
- designing experiments
- selecting datasets and measurements
- defining study protocol
### Project Administration
Managing and coordinating the research project.
Examples:
- tracking progress
- coordinating collaborators
- managing timelines
- communicating with journals and institutions
### Resources
Providing mterials, equipment or infrastructure needed for the research.
Examples:
- providing datasets
- maintaining equipment
- supplying computing resources
- managing storage and infrastructure
